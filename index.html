ABOUT

The club was established by students and faculty members of Civil engineering department IIT Patna in the initial days of setting up of the branch.The club aims for the overall progress of students and help them to excel in all fields and also to improve and increase Student-Faculty interaction.
We at ACE build a healthy environment for faculty-student interaction, our vitality ranges from ‘Neev’, the freshers’ welcome to Civil Cricket League; From celebrating Teachers’ Day to bidding a farewell to seniors and much more. With an active involvement of Faculty members we here at ACE are achieving all that we need to be in the growing stage of the department and yet we strive for more.

OBJECTIVE:

The vision is to develop into a strong platform promoting student-faculty interaction outside of the world of regular academics.A forum where people accumulate & share their knowledge, a forum that passes on the baton through the years so that it provides a head start to students entering the department to maximize their time by taking forward successes and learning from mistakes.

EVENTS

HOUSE OF CARDS

Wanna build structures? Can't wait for becoming old enough? Or have the perfect sense and not the material? Then this is for you. House of Cards is a fun event where you can build structures with cards! ACEbrings to you an opportunity to build 3D Structures with Cards in this two round event. Let your passion show its colors and let your talent and creativity be in continuous flow! This is House of Cards!

CAD MAESTRO

Think your designing skills are better than all? Than prove it to us by taking this challenge. Celesta presents CAD Maestro, the design event. Design the CAD model of the proposed problem and send it to us! The best design wins! Problem statement will be released soon, please follow our Facebook page, Twitter handle, official website and official app for further updates.

SPAGARIDGE

The first bridges built by humans were probably just wooden planks or boulders placed on a flowing stream. But today, engineers have built bridges to cross vast stretches of rivers. Before actually constructing a bridge, we need to model it and test its performance in the lab. Spagaridge brings you a chance of showing your engineering skills with noodles! So, get innovative and build your bridge model with this unconventional construction material.

CRICKET LEAGUE

Every year we at ACE conduct a cricket tournament for the members of DCEE so that they can be a part of sport. Here all the members of the DCEE take part and enjoy the weekend by playing cricket. The tams are a combination of Faculty, B.Tech and M.Tech Students.

FOOTBALL LEAGUE

Every year we at ACE conduct a football tournament for the members of DCEE so that they can be a part of sport. Here all the members of the DCEE take part and enjoy the weekend by playing football. The tams are a combination of Faculty, B.Tech and M.Tech Students.
